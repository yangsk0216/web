Fast food restaurants need to consider many and human factors, such as the cultural background of the population, population density, pedestrian flow, etc. population density is a very important consideration. In densely populated areas, there are more potential customers and therefore the store may have higher traffic and sales. It may be more advantageous to open a store in densely populated areas such as city centers and business districts because they are easily accessible, have high foot traffic, and are more likely to be found by target consumers.
